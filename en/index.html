ENGLISH Gatineau-Ottawa Agile Tour 2015.

Agile Tour


Agile Tour is a series of non-profit events over several cities throughout October to December.

Join us for the 2015 edition of the Gatineau-Ottawa Agile Tour, affectionately known as GOAT. Organized by a local team of dedicated Agile practitioners, #GOAT15 will be an value-packed conference for the 300+ professionals expected to attend this year.

Keynote speakers


Christopher Avery

Christopher is recognized world-wide for his deep expertise and mastery of The Responsibility Process™ — the only how-to-approach for understanding, taking, and teaching personal responsibility, the first principle of success in any endeavor. A sought-after speaker, author, and business advisor on responsible leadership, teamwork, and agile change for companies like GAP, Wells Fargo, and eBay, Christopher is known for his cutting-edge work to demystify and then develop practical team leadership skills for engineers and other technical professionals. He wrote the popular classic Teamwork Is An Individual Skill for everyone who is fed up with working in bad teams. He is the visionary force behind The Leadership Gift™ Program and its growing worldwide community of accredited leaders and coaches. Website: www.christopheravery.com Twitter: @christopheraver

Richard Cheng

Richard is a Certified Scrum Trainer (CST) and Project Management Professional (PMP) with deep expertise in bringing Agile to commercial, non-profit, and US Federal clients. He leads Excella Consulting’s Agile Training program training, coaching, and mentoring clients around Washington, D.C. on the adoption and implementation of Agile and Scrum. Website: www.excella.com Twitter: @RichardKCheng
